Lome Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Lomé Chamber of Commerce was selected as the name because it reflects the focus on fostering economic growth and supporting businesses in Lomé, the capital city of Togo.

Site Purpose

  • Promote the interests of Lomé's business community by fostering networking opportunities and providing resources for growth.
  • Attract tourism and new businesses to Lomé by showcasing the city’s opportunities, culture, and attractions.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber host this month for business networking?
  • Where can I find information about joining the chamber?
  • What are the key attractions and statistics about Lomé?

Color Schema

The following colors were selected for the site:

  • Blue (#004080): Used for headings and the navigation bar to represent professionalism and trust.
  • Light Gray (#f8f9fa): Used as a background color for a clean and modern look.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure readability.
  • Georgia: Used for headings to add elegance and contrast.
